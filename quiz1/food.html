Top 3 Medan Local Food

1. Lontong Medan


Lontong Medan usually consists of lontong made of rice, dry chili sauce which is a combination of peanuts, tempeh and anchovies. In addition, Lontong Medan also contains curry from pumpkin vegetable mixed with carrots and long beans.

This special food menu also includes long bean tauco which can be combined with peeled clams or chicken offal, such as chicken liver and intestines cut into small pieces. Some people also like to serve this lontong with noodle sticks cooked with spices and a mixture of cabbage or just boiled. Lontong Medan is also more delicious when added with boiled eggs.

2. Mie Balap


Mie balap is a food made from noodles that is popular in the city of Medan. Almost on every street corner, there are many sold in the city of Medan. Mie balap is one of the breakfast menus in the city of Medan. Mie balap contains vermicelli and kwetiaw fried with eggs, mustard greens, onions, scallions in large portions. Racing noodles are also topped with fish balls, chicken meat or seafood such as shrimp and squid.

Starting from Jalan Serdang Gang Sado, Mie balap spread throughout the city of Medan. Called Mie balap because the process when the chef cooks it really fast like a race and directly in large quantities. In general, the noodles served are halal.

3. Bika Ambon


For those who don't know it, they might think this one food comes from Ambon. However, actually Bika Ambon is a typical Medan food. Bika Ambon was first produced on Jalan Ambon, Medan City. Made from ingredients such as eggs, sugar, and coconut milk, Bika Ambon is well known in Indonesia.

After the ingredients are mixed, the Bika ingredients will be fermented for seven hours by mixing sap water (air nira). This sap water sensation is what makes this food so unique in North Sumatra.
